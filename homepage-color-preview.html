Morgan Signal Layer

Morgan should feel like the advisor behind the center.

This section introduces Morgan without putting a chat box on the homepage. It creates presence, trust, and anticipation for the post-assessment session.

Morgan does not interrupt the first visit. Morgan receives the report.

The stronger message is that Morgan is not a homepage widget. Morgan is the advisor waiting inside the office after the assessment is complete, ready to interpret the report and route the member.

Service Blueprint

Build the lower homepage around what NEBC actually does.

This is the practical section that explains the value without looking like standard SaaS cards. It gives us a structured blueprint we can convert into the real lower homepage once approved.

NEBC Lower Homepage Blueprint

Assessment → Report → Morgan → Department
MODULE A

What happens first

The assessment collects the minimum information needed to understand the business stage and service need.

MODULE B

What gets created

The report produces a readiness score, missing items, priorities, 30-day action plan, and business plan.

MODULE C

Who reviews it

Morgan opens after the report and begins the post-assessment advisory session.

MODULE D

Where the user goes

The member is routed into the right department based on readiness, not random navigation.

MODULE E

What departments provide

Each service unit must have purpose, intake, workflow, deliverables, and acceptance standards.

MODULE F

Why it feels premium

The site communicates discipline, clarity, protection, and execution — not feature clutter.
